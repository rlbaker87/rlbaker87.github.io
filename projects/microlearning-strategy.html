The Challenge

Establish organization-wide microlearning standards and capabilities where none existed. The initiative required building a practical toolkit, developing departmental standards, and designing a rollout strategy that would integrate into existing workflows without creating additional burden on teams.

Solution

  • Developed a Microlearning Toolkit with templates, design guidelines, and best practices ready for immediate use across the department
  • Created departmental standards documentation to ensure consistent quality and approach across the organization
  • Designed an implementation strategy built around existing frameworks and workflows to reduce change management friction
  • Led a five-person deployment team through strategy development and leadership review

Impact and Results

Strategy approved by leadership for enterprise-wide deployment. The toolkit and standards are ready for immediate use. By integrating into existing frameworks, the approach reduces upskilling burden on teams while building organizational capability in modern learning delivery methods.

Role: Strategy Lead and Framework Developer
Tools: Microlearning Design Principles, Change Management, Toolkit Development, Organizational Standards Documentation, Workflow Integration
