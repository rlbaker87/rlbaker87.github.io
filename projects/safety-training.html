The Challenge

Create effective safety training for healthcare team members across a range of high-risk environments — from warehouse chemical handling and kitchen fire safety to treating contaminated patients. Content had to meet OSHA, DHEC, and FEMA standards while remaining practical and immediately actionable in situations where errors have serious consequences.

Solution

  • Developed First Receiver training for healthcare team members responding to contaminated patients across HAZMAT, biological, chemical, radiological, and explosive threat scenarios
  • Created warehouse safety training covering chemical handling, equipment operation, and lifting protocols
  • Designed kitchen fire safety and emergency response protocols for food service environments
  • Partnered with field teams to validate content against real-world performance needs across each operational setting
  • Integrated OSHA, DHEC, and FEMA requirements throughout using scenario-based design to support practical application

Impact and Results

Improved safety compliance and reduced incident reports across multiple high-risk environments. Field-validated content ensures team members are prepared for situations ranging from routine safety protocols to emergency response for contaminated patients.

Role: Instructional Designer and Safety Training Specialist
Tools: Scenario-Based Learning Design, Regulatory Compliance Integration (OSHA, DHEC, FEMA), Field Validation, Camtasia, Articulate Storyline 360
