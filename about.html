My Approach

I am driven by curiosity and the challenge of getting to the root of a problem. When organizations face learning challenges, I do not just design training. I analyze the system, identify what is actually blocking performance, and build solutions that connect directly to business outcomes.

My background spans enterprise instructional design, a decade of classroom teaching and collegiate instruction, and years of athletic coaching. Each of those roles taught me something different about how people actually learn, what gets in the way, and what it takes to move performance. I brought all of that into corporate learning when I joined Prisma Health in 2022, and it shapes every project I take on.

At Prisma Health, I design and lead learning initiatives for a 30,000+ employee organization across South Carolina and Tennessee. Recent work includes building Forge Coach, the organization's first AI-integrated leadership training experience, and serving as Project Manager for the annual Learning and Leadership Symposium, a 400+ attendee enterprise event with 30+ cross-functional team members. I also serve as an AI Champion, helping colleagues build practical fluency with tools that are reshaping how we work.

I believe AI should enhance human expertise, not replace it. That principle guides every AI-integrated learning experience I design.

Professional Journey

I spent a decade in education, teaching high school and collegiate students, designing professional development for educators, and coaching wrestling and football. That work built my foundation in curriculum design, facilitation, performance analysis, and developing people under pressure. I learned how to break down complex concepts, adapt to wildly different audiences, and use data to improve outcomes.

In 2022 I transitioned to enterprise instructional design at Prisma Health, bringing that same analytical and results-focused approach to a much larger stage. Over the past three years I have progressively taken on more strategic scope, from designing individual learning solutions to leading enterprise-wide initiatives, managing cross-functional teams, and pioneering AI integration in learning design.

Today I lead projects that span AI-integrated leadership development, enterprise compliance training covering federal healthcare statutes, high-risk environment safety training, and large-scale program management. I am particularly energized by work that sits at the intersection of technology and human performance, where the design challenge is not just what to teach but how to make it stick in the real world.
