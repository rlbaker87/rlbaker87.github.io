My Approach

Learning never stops for me—it's not just what I do, it's who I am. I'm driven by curiosity and the challenge of getting to the root of a problem. When organizations face complex learning challenges, I don't just design training; I analyze the system, identify what's actually blocking performance, and create solutions that deliver measurable business results.

My approach combines strategic thinking with practical execution. I've pioneered AI-integrated leadership development, optimized enterprise compliance training across two states, and led learning initiatives reaching 40,000+ team members. But what matters most isn't the scale—it's the impact. Every project I take on is guided by one question: How does this help people perform better and drive organizational goals?

Professional Journey

I've spent over a decade in learning and development, with the last three years focused on enterprise instructional design at Prisma Health, a 40,000+ employee healthcare organization spanning two states.

My background includes 10 years in education—teaching at the high school and collegiate level, designing professional development for educators, and coaching teams to measurable improvement. That foundation taught me how to break down complex concepts, adapt to diverse audiences, and use data to drive results. When I transitioned to corporate learning in 2022, I brought that same analytical, results-focused approach to enterprise-scale challenges.

Today, I lead strategic initiatives like our annual Learning & Leadership Symposium (400+ attendees, $60K+ budget), pioneer AI-integrated learning solutions, and develop training that spans everything from regulatory compliance to high-risk emergency response. I'm particularly energized by projects that push boundaries—whether that's building custom AI agents, designing universal training frameworks across state lines, or creating learning experiences that blend technology with human connection.
