AI-Integrated Learning

Forge Coach: Prisma Health's First AI-Integrated Leadership Training

Designed and built a custom Microsoft CoPilot coaching agent for Prisma Health's annual Learning and Leadership Symposium, the organization's first AI-integrated leadership training experience. The 60-minute session combined a Lego-based team challenge with real-time AI consultation, serving director-level and above leaders.

Forge Coach operates on a Socratic methodology, guiding leaders to think through strategic communication challenges rather than providing direct answers. Built using advanced prompt engineering and tested across multiple AI platforms including ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity before deployment.

Impact: Rated 4.7/5 by attendees. Now serves as the departmental case study for AI-enhanced learning design and the model for future AI-integrated initiatives across the enterprise.
