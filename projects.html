AI-Integrated Learning

Forge Coach: Prisma Health's First AI-Integrated Leadership Training

Designing and building a custom AI coaching agent for senior healthcare leaders at the annual Learning and Leadership Symposium

The Challenge

Design a 60-minute leadership development experience for director-level and above healthcare leaders that builds practical AI fluency while developing strategic communication and problem-solving skills, without it feeling like a technology training session.

Solution

  • Built a custom Microsoft CoPilot agent (Forge Coach) using a hybrid prompt architecture designed around Socratic coaching methodology
  • Used ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity to build, stress-test, and refine the agent before deployment
  • Designed a hands-on Lego-based crisis simulation where teams consult Forge Coach in real time to work through strategic communication challenges
  • Developed the "5 P's of Prompting" framework (Prime, Persona, Privacy, Product, Polish) as a transferable skill tool for leaders
  • Facilitated the session alongside department leadership for an audience of senior healthcare leaders

Impact and Results

4.7/5 Attendee Rating

Model Departmental Case Study for AI-Enhanced Learning Design

Demonstrated how AI can enhance human collaboration and strategic thinking rather than replace it. The session now serves as the departmental model for future AI-integrated learning initiatives across the enterprise.

Role: Lead Designer and AI Integration Specialist
Tools: Microsoft CoPilot Agent Development, ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Prompt Engineering, Experiential Learning Design, Lego Serious Play Methodology

Enterprise Learning

Annual Compliance Training Redesign

Redesigning mandatory compliance training for 30,000+ team members across two states while maintaining full regulatory standards

The Challenge

Redesign annual compliance training reaching 30,000+ team members across South Carolina and Tennessee, covering federal statutes including HIPAA, EMTALA, Stark Law, and the Anti-Kickback Statute. Content needed to work across state-specific regulatory requirements using universal, evergreen language while reducing completion time and maintaining full audit readiness.

Solution

  • Audited existing content for inefficiencies and eliminated redundancy while preserving compliance integrity across both state operations
  • Developed a universal language framework covering HIPAA, EMTALA, Stark Law, Anti-Kickback Statute, and information blocking standards applicable across state lines
  • Redesigned course navigation for a more intuitive learner experience and faster content access
  • Used AI tools including ChatGPT, Claude, and CoPilot to accelerate content development while maintaining quality and regulatory accuracy
  • Added interactive design elements to improve engagement and support knowledge retention

Impact and Results

Achieved measurable reduction in training completion time across the enterprise, generating meaningful productivity savings for 30,000+ team members. Maintained 100% regulatory compliance and audit readiness throughout. The universal language framework ensured consistent learning outcomes across both state operations without requiring separate content tracks.

Role: Co-Lead Instructional Designer
Tools: Articulate Storyline 360, Articulate Rise, ChatGPT, Claude, Microsoft CoPilot, Content Analysis, Compliance Framework Development

High-Risk Training

Safety and Emergency Response Training

Designing life-critical training across high-risk environments from warehouse operations to contaminated patient care

The Challenge

Create effective safety training for healthcare team members across a range of high-risk environments, from warehouse chemical handling and kitchen fire safety to treating contaminated patients. Content had to meet OSHA, DHEC, and FEMA standards while remaining practical and immediately actionable in situations where errors have serious consequences.

Solution

  • Developed First Receiver training for healthcare team members responding to contaminated patients across HAZMAT, biological, chemical, radiological, and explosive threat scenarios
  • Created warehouse safety training covering chemical handling, equipment operation, and lifting protocols
  • Designed kitchen fire safety and emergency response protocols for food service environments
  • Partnered with field teams to validate content against real-world performance needs across each operational setting
  • Integrated OSHA, DHEC, and FEMA requirements throughout using scenario-based design to support practical application

Impact and Results

Improved safety compliance and reduced incident reports across multiple high-risk environments. Field-validated content ensures team members are prepared for situations ranging from routine safety protocols to emergency response for contaminated patients.

Role: Instructional Designer and Safety Training Specialist
Tools: Scenario-Based Learning Design, Regulatory Compliance Integration (OSHA, DHEC, FEMA), Field Validation, Camtasia, Articulate Storyline 360
