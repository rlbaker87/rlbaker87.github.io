AI-Enhanced Learning

Innovation Forge: AI-Powered Leadership Development

Pioneering AI integration in healthcare leadership training through custom chatbot development and experiential learning design

The Challenge

Design a 60-minute leadership development experience for senior healthcare leaders (Directors and above) that teaches practical AI skills while developing strategic communication and problem-solving capabilities—all without feeling like a technology training session.

Solution

  • Built custom AI agents in Microsoft CoPilot with hybrid prompt architecture, creating "Forge Coach" as an intelligent thought partner
  • Leveraged external AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity) for building and testing the AI Agent to ensure robust coaching capabilities
  • Designed hands-on crisis simulation combining traditional team challenges with real-time AI consultation
  • Created "5 P's of Prompting" framework (Prime, Persona, Privacy, Product, Polish) for practical skill transfer
  • Integrated Socratic coaching methodology that guides teams to discover solutions rather than providing answers

Impact & Results

100+ Senior Healthcare Leaders Trained
First AI-Integrated Leadership Training at Prisma Health
Model Case Study for AI Agent Development & Experimental Framework

Pioneered organizational adoption of AI-enhanced learning by demonstrating how AI can augment (not replace) human collaboration and strategic thinking. Now serves as departmental case study for AI agent building and experimental model for future AI-integrated learning initiatives across the enterprise.

Role: Lead Instructional Designer & AI Integration Specialist - Built AI agents and leveraged multiple AI platforms for development and testing
Tools: Microsoft CoPilot AI Agents, ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Custom Prompt Engineering, Experiential Learning Design, Lego Serious Play Methodology

Enterprise Learning

Compliance Training Transformation

Redesigning mandatory training to reduce completion time while maintaining regulatory standards across 40,000+ employees

The Challenge

Redesign mandatory annual compliance training across 40,000+ team members spanning two states with varying regulatory requirements. Content needed to use universal, evergreen language applicable across state lines while reducing completion time, improving engagement, and maintaining all regulatory standards without compromising audit readiness.

Solution

  • Analyzed content inefficiencies and eliminated redundant information while preserving compliance integrity across state-specific requirements
  • Developed universal language framework ensuring content applicability for team members in both states
  • Redesigned navigation architecture for intuitive user experience and faster content access
  • Leveraged AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, CoPilot) to accelerate content development workflows while maintaining quality
  • Implemented interactive design elements to increase engagement and knowledge retention

Impact & Results

Achieved significant reduction in training completion time across the enterprise, generating substantial productivity gains for 40,000+ team members. Maintained 100% regulatory compliance and audit readiness while improving employee satisfaction with the training experience. Universal language approach ensured consistent learning outcomes across both state operations.

Role: Lead Instructional Designer
Tools: Articulate Storyline 360, AI-Enhanced Development (ChatGPT, Claude, CoPilot), Content Analysis, UX Design, Compliance Framework Development

The Challenge

Content must meet multiple regulatory standards (OSHA, DHEC, FEMA) while remaining accessible and immediately actionable where mistakes have serious consequences.

Solution

  • Designed kitchen fire safety and emergency response protocols for food service environments
  • Collaborated with field teams to assess real-world performance needs across diverse operational settings
  • Integrated OSHA, DHEC, and FEMA requirements with scenario-based practical application
  • Developed multimedia training ensuring consistent safety standards across all high-risk environments

Impact & Results

Improved safety compliance rates and reduced incident reports across multiple high-risk healthcare environments. Enhanced operational efficiency and emergency preparedness through comprehensive, field-validated training that prepares team members for life-critical situations ranging from routine safety protocols to emergency response for contaminated patients.

Role: Instructional Designer & Safety Training Specialist
Tools: Scenario-Based Learning Design, Regulatory Compliance Integration (OSHA, DHEC, FEMA), Field Validation, Multimedia Development (Camtasia, Articulate), Emergency Response Protocols
